## About

I am **Sagar J C**, a Ph.D. student in the Department of Theoretical Physics at the **Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR), Mumbai**.

My research interests lie in **String Theory**, **Conformal Field Theory**, **AdS/CFT correspondence**, **Quantum Gravity**, and **Black Hole Physics**. I am particularly interested in exact worldsheet descriptions of string theory on Group/Coset Manifolds, Wess–Zumino–Witten (WZW) models,  and the role of conformal symmetry in quantum gravity.

Before joining TIFR Mumbai, I completed my M.Sc. in Physics at the **National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK), Surathkal**, where I graduated with a perfect CGPA of **10.0/10.0** and was awarded the **Board Chairman's Gold Medal** and the **K. Subbarayappa Gold Medal** for outstanding academic performance.

My current research is centered on string theory and conformal field theory. My previous research focused on black hole physics, quasinormal modes, and gravitational perturbation theory, culminating in a publication in **Physical Review D** you can find it [here](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevD.111.064034). This website serves as a central repository for my research, publications, teaching, projects, and academic activities.
