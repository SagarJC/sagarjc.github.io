I am **Sagar J C**, a Ph.D. student in the **Department of Theoretical Physics** at the **Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR), Mumbai**.

My research focuses on String Theory, Conformal Field Theory, AdS/CFT correspondence, and Quantum Gravity, with particular interest in exact worldsheet descriptions of strings on group and coset manifolds, Wess–Zumino–Witten (WZW) models, and holography.

This website serves as a central repository for my research, publications, lecture notes, technical reports, teaching activities, and academic achievements.
